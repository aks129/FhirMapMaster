Parker

Your Friendly Healthcare Data Mapper

"Mapping healthcare data with great power and great responsibility!"

Parker is a FHIR mapping tool that transforms healthcare data from various formats (CSV, HL7 v2, C-CDA, JSON, XML) into FHIR R4B resources. It's a Streamlit-based web application with AI-assisted mapping capabilities.
